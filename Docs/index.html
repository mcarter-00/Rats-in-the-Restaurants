Rats in the Restaurant?

Project Description

According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, 1 in 6 Americans (or 48 million people) get sick from a foodborne disease, 128,000 get hospitalized, and 3,000 are killed each year. About half of foodborne-disease outbreaks occur at restaurants and commercial eating establishments.

Los Angeles County instituted the letter grade system to more than 25,000 restaurants and conducts nearly 50,000 restaurant inspections each year.
